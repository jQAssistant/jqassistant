== 01 - Sub Chapter Title

=== Context

Describe the specific technical or business problem that needs a standardized solution. For example, why a specific library or pattern (like SLF4J or Paging) was chosen.

=== Decision

Detail the specific rules or standards to be followed. This often includes:


* **Core Patterns:** Which concepts (e.g., Spring Data or Jakarta EE) the team is orienting toward.

* **Signatures/Structures:** The exact order of parameters or components.

** *Parameter 1* (e.g., Context)

** *Parameter 2* (e.g., Filter attributes)

** *Parameter 3* (e.g., Paging/Sorting objects)

* **Definitions:** Breakdown of what specific objects (like PagingParams) consist of (e.g., offset, limit, sortOrder).

=== Notes

* Clarify the scope of the decision (e.g., whether it focuses on Facade methods or Repositories).

* Mention any legacy code considerations or items that should eventually be removed from signatures.

=== Consequences

List the expected outcomes of following this template:

* **Uniformity:** Consistent signatures or patterns across the codebase.

* **Usability:** Easier implementation or consumption of the methods.
